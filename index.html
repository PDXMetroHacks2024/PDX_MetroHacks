PDX MetroHacks 2024


SW Washington’s First & Largest Hackathon!

Date + Location

Columbia Room

Fort Vancouver Libaries, Vancouver WA

9/21/24

10 AM - 4PM (Lunch provided)

What is a hackathon?

Hackathons are more than places for coding nerds to get together. They allow the next generation to build ideas and collaborate with peer.

Innovation is born by practice.

That’s why we set out to create the first hackathon in southwestern Washington.

Build your network at this 8-hour long computer science festival, celebrating the unique talent of the Portland Metro region.

Speak to our guest speakers and experienced volunteers.

Receive special prizes to propel your career in the field.

Who can compete?

  • Students under 18 years of age
  • Anyone with an interest in coding, compsci, and similar fields
  • Anyone who is free from 10 AM to 4 PM on Sept. 21!
